Nathan's Soup and Salad

Alert: Nathan's does not accept Meal Exchanges

Hours

Weekdays: 10am to 6pm

Saturday: 11am to 2pm

Description

Located in the Student Alumni Union, Nathan's Soup and Salad is a local Rochester favorite since 1980. Nathan's offers a variety of healthy home-style soups, sandwiches, and fresh-baked breads. Original old-fashioned recipes are mixed with new recipes to offer the perfect meal.
