Description

Located in Grace Watson Hall, Gracie’s is our largest dining facility on campus, serving all-you-care-to-eat. Gracie's is a peanut and tree nut free facility offering Mongolian Grill, Plant Based (vegan) station, brick oven pizza, pasta, carving station, grill, made to order deli, salad bar, waffle station, dessert area with fresh baked cookies, and many beverage choices.
